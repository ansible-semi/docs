Ansible vs Semi
###############

Whenever you encounter an issue when using Semi - you should find out if the **error is caused by Semi or your Ansible** environment.

Many reported issues have nothing to do with Semi. As **we neither have time, nor indention to act as general Ansible support** - we might not be able to help you in that case.
